About me

    Hongyang Pan, born on Dec. 20th, 1997, received the B.S. degree in electronic and information engineering and the M.S. degree in electronic science and technology in the group of Prof. Zhufei Chu, both from Ningbo University, Ningbo, China, in 2019 and 2023, respectively. He is currently a full professor at Ningbo University, Ningbo. Now I'm pursuing my Ph.D.degree in electronic science and technology in Fudan University, Shanghai, China. His current research interests include many aspects of logic synthesis and its applications.
